About Robyn Plocher, M.Div.

I would like to introduce my photography page. During the years when I worked exclusively with grieving and traumatized children, I started collecting photographs of beautiful things. I did this to counter the heartache and sometimes ugliness I witnessed daily in the children’s stories. The only criterion for “beautiful” was that it gave me joy. Since that time, I have continued to hone my photography skills.

Inspired by Valerie Isenhower, who teaches photography as a spiritual practice, I have come to understand and appreciate each photograph as a gift received rather than an image captured. In that spirit, I would like to share my gifts with you. Feel free to copy and use these images at your discretion. All I ask is that you include the following permission statement near the image: © Robyn J. Plocher. Greeting cards featuring my photography are available for sale at Books and Bakery, 513 Adams St. Carroll, IA 51401.

Please visit the Share the Hope Facebook page. https://www.facebook.com/hope4always/ I would love to hear how you are using my photos.
