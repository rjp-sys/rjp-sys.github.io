About Robyn Plocher, M.Div.

Robyn J. Plocher, M.Div. is an ordained clergywoman in the United Methodist Church. Robyn has over forty years' experience working with grieving and traumatized people of all ages. Robyn was the Founding Director of Rick's House of Hope for Grieving and Traumatized Children of the Iowa Quad Cities (now called Rick's Rays of Hope.) She has also earned multiple professional certifications. Though retired, Robyn continues to find ways to reach out to people in need of hope and encouragement.
