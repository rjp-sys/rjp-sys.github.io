Sample Topics:

Healing The Healer: Preventing Compassion Fatigue

Caregiving professionals of every ilk will appreciate the practical help for preventing and coping with burnout.

Too Tired to Care: Compassion Fatigue and Ministry Professionals

70% of pastors do not have someone they consider to be a close friend.
Only 1 out of 10 pastors will retire as a pastor. Come to help yourself or come to support a pastor you care about.

Source: https://www.pastoralcareinc.com/statistics/

Grief and Faith in the Family

A faith-based exploration of grief's impact on every member of the family.
Attendees will receive coaching on self-care and the needs of the entire family during times of loss.

Helping a Friend Who Is Grieving

A "nuts n bolts" presentation to help you be a better friend to someone who is grieving.
A serious subject presented in a light-hearted and memorable program.

Grief Support Group Facilitation

Certified by The Center for Loss and Life Transition (Alan Wolfelt, Ph.D., Director) Robyn facilitates groups that are part support group and part book study. For adults morning the loss of a love one.
