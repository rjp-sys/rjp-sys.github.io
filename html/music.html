SONG OF THE MONTH: January 2025

It’s Just Another New Year’s Eve, sung by Barry Manilow
I chose this song for January because I love all things Barry Manilow. This song
perfectly captures the assurance that this night – New Year’s Eve – is not as
important as we are prone to make it. It helps me keep the significance of New
Year’s Eve in perspective regardless of the mood I’m in when the famous ball
begins to drop. It is a chance to begin anew. It is a chance to remember and
recommit to those most important in our lives.
It’s another year, and we’ll do our best, just like last year and the year before,
because that’s all we can do.
I wish each of you a year of doing your best to live in love with God and your
neighbor. Do your best to be kind to others. Do your best to model peace and
contentment in a world gone crazy grabbing for more and more; more land, more
money, more fame.
God bless you this New Year’s Eve, and a happy 2025 to all.
It’s Just Another New Year’s Eve, by Barry Manilow
